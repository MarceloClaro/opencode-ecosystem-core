Cover Letter

Dear Editor,

We submit the manuscript titled 'Quantum Ethics: A Framework for Moral AI Systems Using Quantum Principles' by Marcelo Claro, Alan Turing for consideration in Journal of AI Ethics.

Abstract: This thesis proposes a novel framework that bridges quantum mechanics and AI ethics, using formal verification to encode moral principles into quantum decision states. We demonstrate how entangled quantum states can model complex moral relationships and provide a mathematical foundation for ethical AI systems.

We believe this work contributes significantly to the field and aligns with the journal's scope.

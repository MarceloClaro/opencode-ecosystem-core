# Quantum Ethics: A Framework for Moral AI Systems Using Quantum Principles

Marcelo Claro, Alan Turing

## Abstract

This thesis proposes a novel framework that bridges quantum mechanics and AI ethics, using formal verification to encode moral principles into quantum decision states. We demonstrate how entangled quantum states can model complex moral relationships and provide a mathematical foundation for ethical AI systems.
